There’s a crazy story behind this, I had mailed professor Mario krenn at max plank months ago about meeting over a coffee and I suppose he got busy and forgot about it, didn’t seem very interested anymore. 

So I decided to knock his door and tried to talk to him despite the security measures, he seemed startled when I said I want to meet but didn’t have an appointment. He said it doesn’t work like this. On my way home back, I would have walked for 5 mins, I realised I can follow back on the mail where he mentioned about the meeting. He replied, “we can meet later today, at 5.30 if that works for you” he was confused as he didn’t have meetings that day.

I walked back and decided to wait in the lobby, I started reading Alchemist, felt very unique emotion when encountering this para, I felt like I’m the shepherd and max plank was the shop and had to wait  reading a book until professor is free. This is how I fall in love with this book and things like this kept happening throughout.
