Have you ever felt lost in life? Even though everything is going well in your
life, it feels empty and meaningless then you've found the right page.

I have been through existential crisis in my bechlors, I went in search on finding 
[my purpose](/odysseys/purpose/). Before we get started, let me tell you a story 
of a drunken sailor.

> story of drunk sailor by richard hamming

idea is to have flexible system which provides you with sense of direction in all 
areas of your life and reflect when you're lost, asking your reassurence if what 
you truly believed gives meaning to your life stays or your learned something new 
about life and want to upgrade.

It's not always about having a good balanced life, first thing I read was 
[Life advice](https://fs.blog/hunter-s-thompson-to-hume-logan/) Hunter S. Thompson 
gave to his friend Hume Logan, and later when I went through how he lived his life
on wiki I was shocked that he lived such a life, I wasn't able to convience myself 
that both are same person.

What I want to get to is, it's worth living an intentional life, choose to have
your own standards and values and soon you would find it easy to navigate through 
life.

Good starting point is to look at fundamental needs humans have, maslow and trancend.
I would suggest checking out few frameworks or build your own and see what you like,
pick one and stick to it.
