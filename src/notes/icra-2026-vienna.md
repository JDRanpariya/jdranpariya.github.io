ICRA 2026 runs June 1–5 at VIECON in Vienna. The official site scatters information across a dozen pages and two separate Google Docs that aren't linked prominently anywhere. This is everything in one place so you can actually decide what to attend without 15 browser tabs open.

Within each day, parallel events are grouped by their start time. If three things start at 09:00, you'll see all three together so you can pick.

## Vienna survival

**Tap water** is alpine spring water. Better than bottled. Refill at fountains in Hall A and the Mall. Free NOKOV water bottle at Foyer A (first-come).

**Cash** needed at some restaurants. Gasthaus Rohrböck (Schnitzel) and Cordon Vert are cash-only.

**Airport transit**: trains every 30 min to Wien Mitte or Praterstern (one stop from venue). CAT train 25% off with code . 7-day Vienna pass: €25.20.

**Thursday = public holiday**: shops closed except Praterstern station. Restaurants open but make reservations. Plachutta (Tafelspitz), Drei Hacken (bone marrow).

**Device charging**: area in Hall B between competitions. Power sockets + lockable charging boxes.

**Emergency**: European 112 · Ambulance 144 · Fire 122 · Police 133
